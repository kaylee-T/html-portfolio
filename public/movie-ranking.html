This movie is such a journey for me. I feel the main characters convictions as well as his loss and pain. And I feel the daughter's loss and pain as well as her immense drive to prevail against impossible odds. Also the music is amazing.

The Office

While not a movie, this is one of my favorite "Netflix and Chills". Watching this show always puts me in a relaxed state of mind, and it's incredibly funny.
